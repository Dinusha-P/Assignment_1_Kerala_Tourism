Districts of Kerala

Kerala is a state located in Southern part of Inida. It is the 23rd-largest state by area covering an area of 38,863 square km, with population of 33,406,061 inhabitants. It is bordered by Tamil Nadu to the south and east, Karnataka to the north and the Arabian Sea to the west. The state of Kerala is divided into 14 districts. Thiruvananthapuram is the capital of Kerala.
